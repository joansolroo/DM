                                                dovyski bob • 2 years ago

                                                Thank you! Yes, it can be extended to have something like that. When you are about to apply the steering force to the velocity vector, you can make a copy of the current velocity (say "velocity_before").

                                                After you add the steering force, compare "velocity_before" with the updated velocity. If the angle between them is greater than max_turn_rate, it means the last steering force addition produced an "illegal" turn. If that happens adjust the current velocity accordingly (rotating it, for instance).
